About me

I am currently a R&D software engineer at Siemens Digital Industries Software, in the team of Scott Canann. I am in charge of surface quad meshing & high quality triangulation for the CFD software Star-CCM+.
I earned my Ph.D. in computer science, Polycube Dominant Meshing, in Team Pixel under the supervision of Dmitry Sokolov, Nicolas Ray and Franck Ledoux. It won the second place in the French Computer Graphics Association's Ph.D. award.
